Welcome!

Hello and welcome to the management software for Jyaasa Creative Group Pvt. Ltd.! Here, you can easily manage student registrations for art classes and general tuition.

In this application, you'll find the following sections:

  • Art Form: Enroll students in art classes, upload photos, and choose class plans.
  • Tuition Form: Manage tuition details for students.
  • Art Students: View a list of all registered art students.
  • Tuition Students: View a list of all registered tuition students.

Feel free to navigate through the tabs to manage student details efficiently.
